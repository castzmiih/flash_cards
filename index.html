What is the difference between the present perfect tense and the past simple tense in English?

The present perfect tense is used to describe actions that happened at an unspecified time in the past or actions that have an effect on the present. For example: I have eaten breakfast. The past simple tense is used to describe actions that happened at a specific time in the past. For example: I ate breakfast at 7 AM.
